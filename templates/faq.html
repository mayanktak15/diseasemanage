How do I submit a consultation form?

Submitting a consultation form is quick and easy:

  1. 1 Log in to your Docify Online account
  2. 2 Navigate to your Dashboard
  3. 3 Fill out the consultation form with detailed symptoms
  4. 4 Click "Submit" and wait for our medical team's response

You can also update or edit your past submissions anytime!
